The are you hungry yet was always half unexpected.
It seemed to know it was due on lunch time but was always asked at a different particular time.
Almost as if the person asking slowed it down. Ive always thought a battle was fought everyday between worry and helplessness- it was what is considered a formality- since i never took it too seriously.

I imagine my relatives thought of me as a neo human capable of living by some sort of super resource efficient tech, but not robotic enough to get rid of the question altogether.

The are you hungry yet was so hard to ask it shapeshifted the faces of those who dared,
separating the eyes from the mouth, each going into a different emotional place.

Truth is it was equally difficult to answer as well.
Sometimes my no, not yet was innocent and well intended,
other times my mouth and eyes divorced each other.
Each going into a different level of starving. “No, not yet” and my eyes salivated traumatized.
