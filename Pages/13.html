I stood there.
My unimportant office as the Guggenheim.
The geometric made sense as soon as my peripheral ears paid slight attention to the rain. The rhythm as aid.

I once heard a critique towards the modern abstracts. In her defense, her heart gets in the way to her possible career as an art dealer.
“Art is time. Only patience can showcase love, since the artist is determined to love its piece”

For me the concept is so stimulating, when the material outcomes im already satisfied and look at it as an uneventful but well received consequence. Even experiencing the material is a blurry stare in which i wander off to the intellectual dimension.

Kandinsky on the other hand was probably just autistic and synesthetic.
But beatifying the internal necessity. Thats love as well.
Thats venerating the intention. To respect it and bring it to life so that its relieved of its torture.
Thats love as well.
