1. New phone in hand

For a second i actually believed my life was going to depend on me understanding the structural language of photography. I cant say i got any closer to.
What actually happened is i lost my eyes. Anytime my brain would register a thing it would violate my eyes entirely and go straight to the steady hand-holding procedure.
When people share their love for “the small things” what they actually mean is a need to belong to a cultural group whose only rules of admission are some loosely picked set of aesthetics.
I never paid attention to the damn small things, they were yelling so loud i could barely concentrate.
Nowadays cute memories packaged in 4:3. Back then, involuntarily invented narrative in the most dormant places.
